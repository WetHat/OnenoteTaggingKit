The OneNote Ribbon Bar

All commands and dialogs of the OneNote Tagging Kit are available in the Home tab of OneNote ribbon bar.

  1. The Tag Pages dialog. Manage Page Tags on OneNote pages (add / remove).
  2. The Update Pages command. Update imported tags and Saved Searches on the current page.
  3. The Find Pages dialog. Find Notes by tag and full-text filters.
  4. The Settings dialog. Manage add-in settings.
